  • Proximity: Related elements, such as product details and CTAs, are grouped together effectively, making it more usable.

Pacific Northwest X-Ray

An online store for x-ray equipment and supplies with an outdated and poorly designed interface.

  • Contrast: Insufficient color contrast makes reading on this site difficult, and too little distinct difference between key elements.
  • Repetition: Scattered usage of font sizes, colors, and design elements disrupt the general cohesion of the site.
  • Alignment: Irregularly aligned text and images make the site disorganized and frustrating to explore.
  • Proximity: Elements are scattered with groupings of unrelated items causing confusing layout.
