1. Project Overview

Purpose of the Website:

This website will serve as a central hub for Brenda (Dobi Daze Instagram) to bring her Instagram followers to a single location where they can see photos from various social media platforms, learn about products she endorses, and view memories related to her dogs.

Intended Users:

The target audience includes Instagram followers, fans of Brenda's dogs, family members, and anyone interested in the dogs' daily life and the products Brenda endorses.

Overview of the Website's Content:

The website will feature the following sections:

  • About Us: Introduction to Brenda and the purpose of the website.
  • Endorsed Products: Information about the products Brenda endorses, with links to purchase.
  • Gallery: A photo gallery showcasing images of Brenda's dogs and their daily lives.
  • Contact Us: A form for visitors to contact Brenda for inquiries.
  • Relaxing Dog Videos: A collection of relaxing and cute dog videos.

5. Page Design

Page Name: Home

Purpose: Provide an introduction to Brenda’s website, explaining the mission and welcoming users.

Audience: Instagram followers, family, and potential visitors.

Content:

  • Introductory text about the website's purpose.
  • Links to other sections (Endorsed Products, Gallery, Contact Us, Relaxing Dog Videos).
  • Image carousel displaying photos of Brenda's dogs or featured products.

Buttons, Links, Drop-downs: Links to other pages, interactive Image Carousel.

Actions: Clicking on navigation links redirects to the corresponding page.

Page Name: Endorsed Products

Purpose: Provide information about products Brenda endorses.

Audience: Followers interested in purchasing or learning about endorsed products.

Content:

  • List of endorsed products with descriptions and images.
  • Links to purchase or learn more.

Buttons, Links, Drop-downs: "Learn More" buttons for detailed product information, "Buy Now" links to external websites.

Actions: Clicking "Learn More" opens a modal or new page with more details. Clicking "Buy Now" opens an external link to purchase.

Page Name: Gallery

Purpose: Showcase images of Brenda's dogs in a photo gallery.

Audience: Instagram followers, fans of Brenda's dogs, and family.

Content:

  • A grid of images categorized by moments like "Playtime," "Cute Moments," and "Training."

Buttons, Links, Drop-downs: Lightbox functionality for viewing images in full screen, optional Category Filters.

Actions: Clicking on an image opens it in a larger lightbox view. Selecting a category filter refreshes the gallery to display only selected types of images.

Page Name: Contact Us

Purpose: Allow users to send inquiries or messages.

Audience: Followers, potential collaborators, or family members who wish to contact Brenda.

Content: Contact form with fields for name, email, and message.

Form Validation: Email validation, message length validation.

Buttons, Links, Drop-downs: Submit button to send the form.

Actions: Submitting the form triggers validation. If validated, the data is displayed in a confirmation message.

Page Name: Relaxing Dog Videos

Purpose: Provide visitors with relaxing and cute dog videos.

Audience: Followers of Brenda’s Instagram and anyone interested in dog videos.

Content: Embedded videos of Brenda’s dogs in various cute or relaxing moments.

Buttons, Links, Drop-downs: Play buttons for each video, optional Category Filters.

Actions: Clicking a video thumbnail will open and play the embedded video.

6. Dynamic Functionality

The following interactive elements will be implemented using JavaScript/jQuery:

  • Interactive Forms: The contact form will collect user messages with email validation and message length checks.
  • Image Galleries: Lightbox plugin for full-screen image viewing and dynamic gallery filtering by category.
  • Video Player Interactivity: Embedded YouTube/Vimeo videos with play, pause, and volume controls.
  • Category Filters: JavaScript will dynamically filter content in both the Gallery and Relaxing Dog Videos pages.
