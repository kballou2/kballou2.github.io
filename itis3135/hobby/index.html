When To Lift

One of the toughest things to dial in is when you want to go to the gym. For absolute beginners, lifting two or three days a week is the most ideal. This will help your body get acclimated to the initial shock of lifting weights.

Why to Lift

Lifting weights offers significant physical and mental health benefits, from building muscle mass and strength to improving metabolism and physical capabilities. It promotes fat loss, boosting posture, and correcting muscular imbalances, weightlifting becomes a valuable exercise for overall health at any age.
